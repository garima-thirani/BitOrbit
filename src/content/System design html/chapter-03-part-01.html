🔷 RDBMS vs NoSQL

🗄 RDBMS (SQL)

Structured data with relationships. ACID guarantees. Strong consistency. Examples: PostgreSQL, MySQL. Best for: Banking, ERP, systems where data integrity is critical.

📦 NoSQL

Flexible schemas. Horizontal scaling. Various data models (document, key-value, graph, columnar). Examples: MongoDB, Redis, Cassandra. Best for: Real-time apps, large-scale analytics, IoT.
